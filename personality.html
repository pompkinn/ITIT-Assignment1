Personality

The results of the Myers-Briggs test suited me pretty well as I am more introverted, and could relate to the traits of a ‘Virtuoso’ as I prefer to think logically before acting on my emotions. I also prefer to learn visually and physically and am not creative.

These results show that in a team I would think logically, but stick mostly on the observant side of the group as I am more introverted. However I am not afraid to input on group discussions, I preferable would also follow someone else instead of being a leader in a group.

When forming a team I would preferable search for people with respectable leadership, more extroverted than I am so there is a mix of personalities and a sense of creativity to balance out my lack of creativity.
